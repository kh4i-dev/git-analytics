# Defer Multi-Repo Intelligence Until Sync and Identity Foundations Exist

Status: accepted

Git Analytics will keep the active roadmap to Phase 1 Engineering Toolkit, Phase 2 Single-Repo AI Insight Engine, and Phase 3 Hosted/SaaS Foundation. Multi-repo intelligence, workspace-level reports, cross-repo contributor ranking, executive overview, and identity resolution are deferred to a future Operating System update because they require stronger background sync, job status, retry/recovery, rate-limit, tenant isolation, and contributor identity foundations than the current single-repo product needs.
